🔍 REAL WORLD MISSION 2
▶ FIND THESE
  • A ruler OR a long flat stick OR stiff cardboard
  • Something to balance it across — a pencil, stack of 3 coins, a folded eraser
  • Small objects for weight — coins, legos, rocks, batteries
  • Your dad (seriously — go get him)
▶ BUILD IT

Balance the ruler across your pivot point like a seesaw.

Add objects to BOTH ends until it sits perfectly flat and level.

The objects don't have to match. A heavy thing on one side can balance multiple light things on the other.

When it's balanced — count EVERY object you used on BOTH sides combined.

⚖️ HOW MANY OBJECTS TOTAL?
Count every object on BOTH sides.
Enter that total.

Any number from 2 to 20 is valid.
Every balance is different.

🔍 REAL WORLD MISSION 3
▶ FIND THESE
  • One piece of paper
  • A pencil or pen
▶ BUILD THE STAR MAP

Draw a TRIANGLE anywhere on the paper.

Draw a CIRCLE around it so the whole triangle is inside the circle.

Inside the triangle, write down two things:

1. How many SIDES does your triangle have?

2. How many CORNERS does your triangle have?

Both numbers are the same.
That number is the vault code.

▶ WHY IT'S ALWAYS THE SAME

Every triangle ever drawn has 3 sides and 3 corners. Big, small, or skinny. Always 3. You just proved you know the universal rule.
